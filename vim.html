Vim

Vim is a command-line editor that is included with most *nix machines and OSX.

VimTutor

Use it! It guides you through the basic commands of Vim by doing things instead of reading random wikis on the web and trying to cobble together how to do stuff. You can access it by typing vimtutor at the CLI.
